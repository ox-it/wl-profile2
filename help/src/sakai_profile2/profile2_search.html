Profile2: Searching for connections

Profile2 is the social networking tool for Sakai. Using the Profile2 tool, you can create your own personal profile, add an image for yourself, post status updates, search for people with common interests, view their profiles and add them as connections.

In addition, you have a comprehensive set of Privacy controls so you can decide who can see information about you and a Preferences page where you can configure various settings like your Twitter details (if you have a Twitter account), and what emails you'd like to receive from your connections.
